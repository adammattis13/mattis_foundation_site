The Story Behind Our Mission

In Memory of CPT Kevin "KJ" Smith

In 2005, while serving in Baghdad, Iraq, Adam Mattis's platoon was targeted for assassination. When the bomb detonated, CPT Kevin "KJ" Smith made the ultimate sacrifice‚Äîgiving his life to save Adam's.

That act of selfless courage didn't end on a battlefield. It became the foundation for everything we do today. Adam carries KJ's memory forward through this foundation, ensuring that his teammate's legacy of service, sacrifice, and love for others lives on.

"KJ didn't just save my life‚Äîhe gave me a purpose. Every day, I work to honor that sacrifice by creating opportunities for others to serve." ‚Äî Adam Mattis, Founder
